Mary Clarke

Consultant Heamatologist Epsom and St Helier NHS Trust

Dr Mary Clarke consultant haematologist qualified from Charing Cross Hospital. She works at St Helier providing a laboratory and clinical haematology service. She is the lead clinician for the South West sector for the new NHS Sickle Cell and Thalassaemia Screening unit based in the Regional Infant screening unit at Epsom St Helier. Other special interests include undergraduate education.

Mr Abbad Toma

Consultant ENT and Facial Plastic Surgery St Georgeís Hospital NHS Trust

Mr Toma is a consultant Ear, Nose & Throat (ENT) and Facial Plastic Surgeon to St Georgeís Hospital and Kingston Hospital NHS Trusts, and is Lead Clinician for the Ear Nose and Throat department at St Georges Hospital. He gained specialist training in Rhinology and endoscopic sinus surgery at the University of Pennsylvania, USA and Switzerland.

After training with eminent Facial plastic surgeons in the UK He gained further experience in Facial plastic surgery in Switzerland and France as Fellow of the European Academy of Facial Plastic Surgery. His research interests are in Rhinology including rhinitis and advanced endoscopic sinus surgery. His special interests in addition to general adult and paediatric ENT are rhinology including Endoscopic sinus surgery & Facial plastic surgery.

Prof Matt Thompson

Professor in Vascular Surgery St George's Hospital NHS Trust

Matt Thompson qualified from St Bartholomewís Hospital and trained in vascular surgery at Leicester Royal Infirmary and Queen Elizabeth Hospital in Adelaide. He trained in research techniques as a Wellcome Fellow, and has longstanding research interests into the treatment of abdominal aortic aneurysms. He has over 100 peer-reviewed publications. His clinical interests are in all aspects of Vascular Surgery, including arterial and venous disease. Specialised interests include minimally invasive Vascular Surgery and the treatment of complex aneurysm disease.

Paul Ursell

Consultant Ophthalmologist Epsom and St Helier Hospital NHS Trust.

Mr Paul Unsell is a consultant opthalmologist at Epsom and St Helier NHS Trust. He graduated from St Maryís in London and wrote his MD thesis at St Thomasí Hospital on cataract surgery. He was a clinical lecturer in opthalmology in Oxford for 5 years and then spent a yearís fellowship at the Lionís Institute in Western Australia specialising in advanced cataract and refractive surgery. His specialist interest is in advanced cataract and refractive surgery and he is in demand as a guest speaker at many international meetings.

David Clough

Charles Rippin and Turner Accountants

David Clough is a partner in Charles Rippin and Turner who specialise in dealing with medical accounts, especially those of General Practitioners. David is the Chairman of Association of Independent Specialist Medical Accountants (aisma) and writes regularly for Doctor Magazine, in addition to lecturing to General Practitioners and GP Registrars.

Sandeep H Cliff

Consultant Dermatologist and Dermsurgeon Epsom and St Helier NHS Trust

His area of interest is the surgical management of dermatological conditions which includes skin cancer as well as non cancerous conditions such as hyperhidrosis and penile dermatosis. His research interests are clinical and include the psychological affect of dermatological conditions on patients. He has in addition, over the passed year lectured internationally on skin cancer and its management.

Sami Farhat

Consultant General & Colorectal Surgeon Epsom and St Helier NHS Trust

Recently appointed Consultant in St Helier, he has been involved in Colorectal surgery for some time. His particular interests and experienc lie in laparoscopic gastrointestinal and colorectal surgery, colorectal cancer, screening and colonscopy.

Jan Walker and Julia Russell

Respiratory Nurse Specialists Sutton and Merton PCT

The aim of the respiratory nursing service is to support patients with lung disease in the community and to be a source of advice to members of the multidisciplinary team.
