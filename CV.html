Mr Abbad Toma Consultant ENT and Facial Plastic Surgery St Georgeís Hospital NHS Trust Mr Toma is a consultant Ear, Nose & Throat (ENT) and Facial Plastic Surgeon to St Georgeís Hospital and Kingston Hospital NHS Trusts, and is Lead Clinician for the Ear Nose and Throat department at St Georges Hospital. Prof Matt Thompson Professor in Vascular Surgery St Georgeís Hospital NHS Trust Matt Thompson qualified from St Bartholomewís Hospital and trained in vascular surgery at Leicester Royal Infirmary and Queen Elizabeth Hospital in Adelaide. His clinical interests are in all aspects of Vascular Surgery, including arterial and venous disease.
